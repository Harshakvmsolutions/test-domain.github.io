About M3M Cuillian

M3M is a well-known real estate developer in India, recognized for consistently delivering top-notch projects. The M3M Group has a rich history of over two decades in the real estate sector. With projects spanning various cities across the country, they have set a benchmark in the industry, focusing on customer satisfaction and top-notch construction quality. The builder has recently launched M3M The Cullinan, a project that boasts innovative design and comfortable living.

M3M The Cullinan is a premium residential development located in the heart of Noida, Sector 94. This project will deliver a luxurious lifestyle to its residents with its high-end amenities and stunning architecture. This project is spread over 12.8 acres of land amidst a prime location of Noida. The amenities at The Cullinan are awe-inspiring and extensive. With clubhouses, a swimming pool, a basketball court, community gardens, jogging tracks, a mini theatre, multipurpose halls, a fitness center, tennis and badminton courts, theme gardens, and much more, it's like having access to everything you could need.

The project consists of 3.5, 4.5, and 5.5 BHK apartments catering to the needs of both individuals and families. The starting price of M3M Cullinan ranges from Rs. 5.97 crores with a 30:40:30 payment plan, making it an attractive option for home buyers. The 3.5 BHK apartments vary from 3270 sq. ft. sq ft, providing ample space for families. These apartments feature stylish interiors, luxurious amenities, and stunning views of the surrounding neighborhood. The 4.5 BHK apartments are available in sizes from 4,315 sq ft, offering residents a luxurious and spacious living experience. These apartments have additional bedrooms, making it an ideal choice for large families or those requiring extra space. The 5 BHK apartments in M3M The Cullinan are the epitome of luxury and opulence.

Starting from size 5935 sq. ft. to 5,250 sq. ft., these apartments offer a lavish lifestyle to residents.

The Cullinan is conveniently connected to major airports, as well as metros like Botanical Garden Metro Station and Okhla Metro Station, and highways such as the Noida-Greater Noida Expressway. Additionally, the Okhla Bird Sanctuary is located just a short distance away from The Cullinan. Moreover, the project is close to a range of social infrastructure facilities, including schools, hospitals, and shopping centers. Investing in M3M The Cullinan comes with several benefits. Firstly, the project has been registered under RERA, which ensures transparency and adherence to regulatory guidelines.
