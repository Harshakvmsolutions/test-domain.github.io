About M3M Cuillian

M3M is a popular Indian real estate developer with a track record of building excellent properties on time. With more than 20 years of property industry experience, the M3M Group has left an unforgettable mark in multiple US towns by focusing on customer satisfaction as well as excellent construction. Their most recent project, M3M The Cullinan, is an outstanding example of its commitment to modern design and luxurious lifestyles.

Situated in Sector 94, M3M, the center of Noida A new home building called The Cullinan is redefining luxury living. This project, which spans 12.8 acres, is positioned to provide inhabitants with luxury and convenience. The property offers luxurious and relaxing living thanks to its lovely design and plenty of luxurious facilities.

M3M with a wonderful selection of facilities, The Cullinan can meet a wide range of lifestyle needs. Clubhouses, a small theater, multipurpose rooms, a gym, tennis and badminton courts, vegetable gardens, running tracks, a swimming pool, and a basketball court are open to residents. Additionally, theme gardens and other recreational facilities create an environment where every necessity and luxury is met.

The project offers a selection of large and luxurious apartments, including 3.5, 4.5, and 5.5 BHK units, to meet the needs of single people as well as families with kids. These homes, which have a 30:40:30 payment plan and start at Rs. 5.97 crores, are an attractive choice for choosy property purchasers. Starting at 3270 square feet, the 3.5 BHK apartments offer lots of room together with stylish design, luxurious facilities, and beautiful neighborhood views. The 4.5 BHK apartments offer an additional living space, with floor plans starting at 4315 square feet, which makes them perfect for a group with people looking for more space. The 5.5 BHK apartments provide individuals with a luxurious way of life and are an excellent example of luxury. Their living areas range from 5250 to 5935 square feet.

Because of its central location, The Cullinan offers excellent connectivity and convenience. Major airports, the Okhla Metro Station, and the Botanical Garden Metro Station are all nearby from it. Major highways, such as the Noida-Greater Noida Expressway, also improve connection. In addition, the Okhla Bird Sanctuary is nearby and offers a calm break from city life. In addition, the project is close to several social infrastructure businesses, such as shopping malls, hospitals, and schools, providing that residents are close to any required services.

Investing in M3M The Cullinan offers several benefits. The project meets with laws and is open because of its RERA registration, which gives buyers peace of mind. M3M The Cullinan, with its luxurious features, excellent location, and wide facilities, is proof of M3M Group's dedication to developing outstanding real estate changes.
