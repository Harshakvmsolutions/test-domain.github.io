About Gaur Island

Gaursons India Pvt. Ltd.'s Golf View Residences in Pari Chowk, Greater Noida, represents a high-end luxury residential project that offers a unique and world-class living experience. Here's a summary of the project's key features:

Natural Beauty: The project is set on a sprawling piece of land that boasts abundant natural greenery, creating a serene and refreshing environment for residents.

Strategic Location: Golf View Residences is strategically located near various essential amenities, including educational institutions, financial hubs, healthcare facilities, and commercial centers. Its proximity to these infrastructure elements makes it an attractive choice for residents.

Exclusive Golf Course: A prominent feature of the project is the in-house golf course, allowing residents to enjoy outdoor sports and recreational activities within the residential premises. Diverse Floor Specifications: Golf View Residences offers a variety of floor specifications, with super areas ranging from 3681.79 to 6011.26 sq ft, catering to the diverse needs and preferences of potential buyers. RERA Compliance: Golf View Residences is RERA (Real Estate Regulatory Authority) compliant, which ensures transparency and adherence to real estate regulations, providing buyers with confidence and security.

In summary, Golf View Residences by Gaursons India Pvt. Ltd. promises a luxurious and modern living experience amidst natural beauty and convenience. Its proximity to key infrastructure elements and the inclusion of exclusive amenities make it an appealing choice for those seeking a premium lifestyle in Greater Noida.
