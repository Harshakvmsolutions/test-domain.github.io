About Elan New Launches

Elan The Mark in Sector 82, Gurgaon, is poised to redefine luxury retail in this dynamic city. Strategically located on Dwarka Expressway, this ambitious project covers 12 acres, offering spacious and exclusive spaces for businesses and shoppers alike. The location boasts excellent connectivity to major areas, proximity to the international airport, and a unique blend of luxury retail and a 5-acre 5-star hotel, creating a vibrant atmosphere for an elevated experience.

Elan the Mark, a huge 10,000 crore project built by the famous Elan Group, aims to entirely change Gurgaon's costly retail environment. There is enough for everyone in this innovative project's retail sector, which can fit any type of business requirements. The lower ground and ground levels are 800–1200 square feet, the first floor is 600–1000 square feet, and the second storey is 400–800 square feet. There are a lot of eateries, theaters, and bars in the third storey, which is entertainment-focused.

Elan The Mark is not just about shopping; it is about creating an immersive experience. A five-star hotel is part of the expansion, adding convenience and refinement and drawing in tourists from around the world. Elan The Mark guarantees to be more than just a shopping destination because of its distinctive blend of upscale shopping and hospitality.

With its contemporary aesthetics and well-thought-out spaces, Elan The Mark's architectural design is sure to wow. Every retail space is planned to optimize accessibility and exposure, allowing businesses to prosper. To improve guests' shopping experiences, the property also has a lot of parking space, cutting-edge security systems, and modern amenities.

Apart from its excellent location and opulent amenities, Elan The Mark places a strong emphasis on environmental responsibility and sustainability. Incorporating energy-efficient systems and green building methods, the project lessens its carbon footprint and promotes environmental health. Elan the Mark is a premium destination that is also socially responsible thanks to its dedication to sustainability.

Elan The Mark in Sector 82, Gurgaon, is perfectly situated to make a major mark on the city's commercial landscape because of its great location, luxurious facilities, and focus on the environment. It provides the perfect combination of goods, entertainment, and hospitality, fulfilling the demands of today's clients and creating a fresh standard for premium shopping in the locality. Elan Group's development is an essential destination for both locals and tourists, as it aims to offer a memorable visit.
