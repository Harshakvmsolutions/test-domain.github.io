About SKA Orion

Learn about SKA Orion, a renowned real estate project in Noida's Sector 143 which provides luxurious residences and successful business opportunities. The location of the Noida Expressway and easy access to the Delhi Metro allow it to have good connectivity to the nearby Jewar and IGI Airports.

Featuring stunning architecture and a vibrant atmosphere, SKA Orion provides a comfortable and stylish lifestyle. In addition to upscale apartments, it includes 22 retail spaces for optimal returns on investment.

The residential apartments have large three-bedroom designs as well as modern facilities including a clubhouse, gym, and swimming pool. This project, which got IGBC approval and RERA registration, guarantees development and peace. The Sky Club on the terrace promises an unforgettable experience.

SKA Orion's Sky Gazing feature enhances the living experience, offering a universe of stargazing. Situated in Noida's Sector 104, this house offers quick access to colleges and universities, shopping centers, medical facilities, and bus stops. It's a great choice for everyone looking for a colorful and healthy lifestyle because it has lots of green space and is pollution-free.
