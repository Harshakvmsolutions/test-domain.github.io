About Godrej Meridien

Welcome Godrej Meridien, a new property complex under development in Gurugram's Sector 106. It is an invention of Godrej Properties and aims for a new modern living. Godrej Meridien scheduled for completion in September 2023, offers a luxurious, helpful, and enjoyable living.

Variety of Residences: Gurugram's Godrej Meridien Sector 106

This project offers a range of options for housing, both villas and flats, that suit different price points and tastes. There is something for everybody at Godrej Meridien, whether you're looking for a big family villa or a comfortable apartment.

RERA Certification:

Godrej Meridien Sector 106 Gurugram is Certified with RERA Regulations. Because Godrej Meridien Sector 106 Gurugram is registered with RERA, accessibility and following the rules are guaranteed. With the registration number RC/REP/HARERA/GGM/2018/05, you can find all the project data on the state RERA portal, giving you investing a piece of mind.

Trusted Builder: Godrej Properties

With years of experience, Godrej Properties is a trusted name in Gurgaon's real estate. They've completed 54 projects, have 24 more in progress, and 52 under construction, showing their commitment to quality and timely delivery.

Key Features of Godrej Meridien Sector 106 Gurugram

  • Various Amenities: Godrej Meridien will offer amenities for different lifestyles, like fitness centers, parks, pools, and recreational areas, ensuring a comfortable urban life.
  • Great Location: Located in Sector 106, Gurugram, it's well-connected to key areas like Dwarka Expressway, IGI Airport, and business centers, keeping you close to the city's action.
  • High-Quality Construction: Built with attention to detail and premium materials, every unit reflects Godrej's commitment to long-lasting quality.
  • Sustainability: Godrej Properties prioritizes eco-friendly practices, making Godrej Meridien energy-efficient and environmentally friendly with green spaces.
