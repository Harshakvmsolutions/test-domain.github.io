About Omaxe

Welcome to Godrej Properties, a renowned real estate developer, proudly presents its latest luxury residential project, "Tropical Isle". Strategically situated in Sector 146, Noida Expressway, this project is designed to captivate both potential homebuyers and investors alike. Recently the developer acquired two land parcels in Sector 146 Noida for the development of this magnificent residential township. With its commitment to excellence and innovation, Godrej Group is creating a world-class living experience for its customers. Tropical Isle is a stunning residential township offering spacious floor plans and beautiful green surroundings to ensure a peaceful experience. It is set to be a magnificent addition to the builder's portfolio. This exquisite residential development offers luxurious 3 BHK and 4 BHK apartments

Situated in the heart of Noida, Godrej Tropical Isle is surrounded by lush greenery and offers a serene and tranquil living experience. The apartments are thoughtfully designed to provide maximum comfort and convenience to its residents. These 3/4 BHK flats are both spacious and well-appointed, boasting modern interiors and high-quality fittings. Each apartment is intentionally designed to allow for maximum natural light and ventilation, resulting in a bright and airy living space. Residents of Godrej Tropical Isle will enjoy a host of world-class amenities, including a clubhouse, swimming pool, gymnasium, landscaped gardens, and children's play area. There are also facilities for indoor and outdoor sports, ensuring that residents have ample opportunities to stay active and engaged.

In addition to the luxurious apartments and top-notch amenities, Godrej Tropical Isle Noida also benefits from its strategic location. It is well-connected to major parts of Noida and enjoys easy access to schools, hospitals, shopping malls, and other essential facilities. The proximity to major transportation hubs makes commuting a breeze for residents. Moreover, the project is well-connected with the upcoming Jewar international airport. With its impeccable design, prime location, and world-class amenities, Godrej Tropical Isle offers a truly exceptional living experience. Whether you are looking for a spacious 3 BHK or a luxurious 4 BHK apartment, this development is sure to exceed your expectations. Don't miss the opportunity to be a part of this prestigious residential community. Contact us today to book your dream home at Godrej Tropical Isle, Sector 146, Noida.
